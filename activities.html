Our Yearly Activities

Our activities are for all W.E.R families. Some activities are fully sponsored by the P.A. through our fundraising campaigns
others require partial family contributions.


We are always in need of parent volunteers for our activities. We cannot have successful activities without your help.
Please submit your email below for our newsletter and to be added to our volunteer contact list.


Students will receive information flyers with all pertinent information ahead of an activity.
See below for some of the activites planned for the upcoming year:

  • Family Apple Picking Trip
  • Holloween Party - Grades 3-5
  • Family Game Nights - Grades K-2; Grades 3-5;
  • Santa's Workshop - Grades PK-6
